# 🎬 MovieLibrary

**A private movie collection for friends and family.**

This is a personal movie library service where you can browse and download movies from my collection. Access is by invitation and requires admin approval.

---

## 🔐 How to Get Access

This service is private and invitation-only. Here's how the process works:

### Step 1: Register
Visit the website and click **"Request Access"** on the home page. You'll need to provide:
- **Your name** (first and last)
- **Your email address**

Once you submit, your registration will be **pending admin approval**.

### Step 2: Wait for Approval
After you register:
- Your request is sent to me (the admin)
- I'll review your registration
- I'll decide whether to **approve** or **reject** your request

### Step 3: Receive Confirmation
**If approved:**
- You'll receive an email with your confirmation token
- Follow the link in the email to activate your account
- You'll be logged in automatically and can start browsing!

**If rejected:**
- You'll receive an email explaining that your request was not approved
- Access is granted only to friends and family

**Important:** Each confirmation token can only be used once and is tied to your email address.

---

## 🎥 What You Can Do

Once your account is activated, you can:

### Browse the Collection
- View all movies in a visual poster grid
- Movies are sorted by recently added by default
- Scroll through the entire collection

### Search & Filter
- **Search by title** — Type to find movies (supports both original and Italian titles)
- **Filter by year** — Select a specific year or range
- **Filter by genre** — Select one or more genres
- **Search by actor/director** — Find movies with your favorite actors

### View Movie Details
Click on any poster to see:
- Large movie poster
- Original and Italian titles
- Release year
- Director and main cast
- Runtime
- Genres
- Plot summary

### Download Movies
- Click the **Download** button on any movie detail page
- Files download directly to your device (no streaming)
- Supported formats: MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WebM

## 💡 Tips for Using the Service

### While Waiting for Approval
- You can check your registration status on the home page
- You'll see "Approval Pending" message with your submitted details
- You'll stay logged out until you activate your account with the token

### Finding Movies
- Use the search bar at the top for quick title searches
- Combine filters: try "Action movies from 2020-2023"
- Don't remember the exact title? Try partial words — the search is fuzzy!

### Download Quality
- All movies are available in their original quality
- File sizes range from 1GB to 50GB depending on the movie
- Make sure you have enough storage space before downloading

### Browser Compatibility
- Works best in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- Enable downloads for the site if prompted
- Large downloads work best on stable Wi-Fi connections

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Private Use Only
- This is a personal collection for friends and family
- **Do not share your account** — access is personal and non-transferable
- **Do not redistribute downloaded files** — personal use only
- Access can be revoked if terms are violated

### Account Management
- Registration is public but requires admin approval
- You must activate your account with the confirmation token
- If you lose your token, contact me to request a resend
- Sessions expire after inactivity; you'll stay logged in via cookies
- Access can be revoked by admin at any time

### Technical Requirements
- Modern web browser with JavaScript enabled
- Sufficient storage space for downloads
- Stable internet connection (especially for large files)
- Valid email address to receive confirmation

### Support
Having issues? Contact me directly and I'll help you out:
- Registration not working? → Check your details are correct
- No confirmation email? → Check spam folder or contact me
- Download failed? → Try again, or try a different browser
- Movie info wrong? → Let me know and I'll fix it
- Lost access? → Contact me to check your account status
